Service Lyrics and Presentation

English, Manglish & Malayalam

The Singapore Cathedral has spent many months creating OpenLP-compatible service lyrics that is free to use for our sister churches.

OpenLP is a worship presentation program. It includes the ability to search through lyrics for certain words, search for Bible verses in the Bible, display lyrics and Bible verses in a friendly way to the congregation. It also includes easy and instant switching between slides, customisable backgrounds, a song database, and video support.
