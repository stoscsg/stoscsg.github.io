OUR MISSION

Martha Mariam Samajam

St. Mary’s League (Martha Mariam Samajam) is an organization for the women of the Indian Orthodox Syrian Church. This organization was derived to enhance and enlighten the spiritual and social growth of the women of the church. The Martha Mariam Samajam acquired its name from one of the most honored saints St. Mary. The St. Mary’s League in Singapore is over fifty years old and is very active spiritual organization of our Cathedral. St. Mary’s League conducts special functions and regular bible studies/prayer meetings at our Cathedral on Friday lead by our vicar.
